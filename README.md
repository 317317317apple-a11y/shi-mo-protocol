# shi-mo-protocol
A wetware kernel patch for Human-OS. Regain root access to your limbic system, manage emotional daemons, and prevent CPU overheating during panic attacks.
# Shi-Mo Model: Emotion Regulation based on Stanislavski System
# 史模：基於史坦尼斯拉夫斯基體系的情緒調節模型

> **Note:** This model focuses on negative emotions, as positive emotions typically do not require regulation.
> **註：** 本模型僅討論負面情緒，因為正面情緒通常不需要被處理。

---

## Core Philosophy (核心概念)

### 1. The Magic "If" & Organic Reality
**1. 魔幻假定 (Magic If) 與有機真實**
Use the "Magic If" ("I am not actually X, but *if* I were...") to trigger imagination and find the **Super-Objective**. This creates an **"Organic Reality"**—not the objective truth of the physical world, but a reality the mind can believe in.
利用「Magic If」（我並不是...但假設我是...）來開啟想像，並藉此找尋超動機。這能創造出「有機真實」——這不是真正世界的真實，而是你腦海能夠相信的真實。

### 2. Shell (Emotion) vs. Soul (Subject)
**2. 殼 (情緒) 與 魂 (本體)**
*   **Concept:** Negative emotions are products of the Subject. They are the **"Shell"** generated to confront threats. Just as skin protects organs, emotion protects the self.
*   **Key Insight:** Since the Subject creates the Product, the Subject is superior. You are not your emotion; you can master it. Do not treat emotion as an enemy (Object) to be annihilated, or you lose your agency.
*   **概念：** 負面情緒是本體的產物（非主非客），是面對威脅而產生的「殼」。正如皮膚保護器官，情緒也是一種保護機制。
*   **關鍵：** 因為是主體的產物，主體的身分遠高於產物。這讓你奪回主導權。不要認為你等於情緒，也不要將情緒視為需要殲滅的客體。

### 3. The King and The Soldiers
**3. 國王與士兵**
*   **Imagery:** Visualize the threat as an attack on a castle. Negative emotions are **Soldiers** automatically generated to defend it. You are the **King** observing from above.
*   **Strategy:** The King does not fight; he rests. Let the soldiers fight where you feel the sensation (skin, head, heart). Once the threat is gone, the soldiers will vanish.
*   **意象：** 透過想像，威脅來臨時就像城堡被攻擊。負面情緒是源源不絕製造出來保衛你的「士兵」。你是城堡上觀戰的「國王」。
*   **策略：** 國王不需要擔心，應該去休息。情緒在哪打架（皮膚、頭、心臟），就放著讓他打。威脅感消失，情緒士兵自然會不見。

## Technical Analysis (技術分析)

### 4. Emotion vs. Affective Memory
**4. 情緒 vs. 情感記憶**
*   **Emotion:** Pure, internal product.
*   **Affective Memory:** A "virus." It is a memory of a past event masquerading as an emotion. It represents a linear history condensed into a point.
*   **情緒：** 純內部的產物。
*   **情感記憶：** 是「病毒」。它是人們對於過去事件的記憶，但在人體內部偽裝成情緒。它看似是一個點，其實代表了那件事的整條線。

### 5. Why Abandon Affective Memory?
**5. 為何放棄情感記憶？**
(Based on Late Stanislavski & Stella Adler)
1.  **Inconsistency:** The same emotion feels different in different memories.
2.  **Depletion:** Recalling trauma is exhausting.
3.  **Distortion:** Memories deviate from truth over time.
基於晚期史坦尼斯拉夫斯基與阿德勒：(1) 相同情緒會有不同的情感記憶；(2) 調動情感記憶消耗巨大；(3) 記憶重組會偏離真實。

### 6. The Solution: Ignore and Deploy
**6. 解決方案：忽略與部署**
*   **Identify:** If a feeling is linked to a past event, it is Affective Memory. **Ignore it.**
*   **Deploy:** Use the **King** to command innate **Emotion Soldiers** to the body part feeling the threat. Recognize them as a protective shell.
*   **識別：** 辨識出情感記憶的事件性，直接忽略它。
*   **部署：** 直接動用國王調動情緒士兵去你感受到威脅的身體部位防守。認知到情緒是保護你的殼。

## Advanced Techniques (進階技巧)

### 7. Method of Physical Action
**7. 形體動作法**
*   **Scenario:** Facing authority/conflict.
*   **Action:**
    *   **Defend:** Command soldiers to the threatened area (e.g., mouth). Order: "Stand ready. Do not attack."
    *   **Gestures:** Point with a finger to direct soldiers. Sit on a high chair (Throne). Close eyes (Thinking).
*   **情境：** 面對權威壓制或衝突。
*   **動作：**
    *   **防守：** 指揮士兵到受威脅部位（如嘴巴）。指令：「整裝蓄勢待發，但不進攻。」
    *   **姿態：** 伸出手指指揮。坐在高位的椅子上。閉上眼睛思考。

### 8. Interpersonal Strategy: Organic Cold War
**8. 人際策略：有機的冷戰**
*   **Strategy:** Do not engage in hot conflict (negative bond), nor total disconnection (internal loop).
*   **Execution:** Deploy soldiers to the boundary (mouth/eyes). Maintain connection/gaze with **commanding presence**. "Defend here. Do not fire."
*   **策略：** 切忌持續對抗（負面連結），也不能單純冷戰（情感記憶影響）。
*   **執行：** 在感受到不舒服的部位部屬士兵防禦。保持眼神交流與氣勢。「在這裡防守，不准進攻。」

### 9. Rhythm & Speed: The Archetypes
**9. 節奏與速度：原型**
*   **Archetypes:** **King** + **Armchair Detective** + **Professor X**.
*   **Reasoning:** To stabilize the frantic soldiers, the commander must be slow, wise, and authoritative.
*   **The "Professor X" Protocol:**
    1.  **Sit Down:** Lock movement (Wheelchair).
    2.  **Touch Temple:** Focus mind (Cerebro).
    3.  **Close Eyes:** Enter Mind Palace.
    4.  **0.3x Speed:** Move in slow motion to force physiological calm.
*   **原型：** 國王 + 安樂椅神探 + X教授。
*   **理由：** 為了穩定急躁的士兵，指揮官必須穩定、有權威感。
*   **X教授協議：** (1) 坐下；(2) 手指按太陽穴；(3) 閉眼；(4) 0.3倍速慢動作。

## Failsafe Modes (故障安全模式)

### 10. Scaling: The Ant and The Thunder
**10. 尺度轉換：螞蟻與雷**
*   **When:** The threat is too huge, and the King cannot be summoned.
*   **Imagery:** Threat = Thunder. You = **Ant**.
*   **Action:** Find a safe space. "How does the Thunder shelter me?"
*   **時機：** 威脅太大，國王喚起失敗。
*   **意象：** 威脅是雷，你是**螞蟻**。
*   **動作：** 找個安全的地方躲起來。

### 11. Depersonalization: The Immune System
**11. 非人原型：免疫系統**
*   **When:** Cannot imagine human roles.
*   **Imagery:** You = **Immune System**. Emotion = **White Blood Cells**.
*   **Action:** Stay still. Secrete cells to the threatened area.
*   **時機：** 無法想像人物時。
*   **意象：** 你是免疫系統，情緒是白血球。
*   **動作：** 靜靜待著，生產白血球到受威脅的地方。

## Conclusion (結語)

> "Emotion cannot be controlled directly, but action can. If you perform the correct action, the corresponding emotion will be triggered." — **Stella Adler**
> 「情緒無法人為控制，行動卻可以，而且如果你正確地執行了行動，就能激發相應的情緒。」—— **史黛拉·阿德勒**

**Key Takeaway:** Use imagination (King/Ant) to trigger physical actions (0.3x speed/Defense), which in turn regulates the emotion.
**總結：** 透過想像（國王/螞蟻）引發行動（0.3倍速/防守），進而調節情緒。
